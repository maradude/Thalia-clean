The Creators of Thalia

Thalia was born as a simple idea, but ended up being the result of many months of hard work. As for any sofware product there is a team of great software engineers behind Thalia, who worked tirelessly to deliver it. This page is dedicated to them.

 

Veronica Kakavou

I was responsible for the development of front end parts of Thalia. I enjoy reading books, travelling around the world, aviation, and catching up with new technologies.

 

Martti Aukia

Team lead/QA/front end dev/tester/occasional bug fixer extraordinaire. You can usually find me sick at home or leaving overly pedantic code reviews.

 

Albert Boehm

Together with Daniel, I was responsible for developing the number crunching aspects of Thalia. A person like many other, I find pleasure in reading books, spending time in nature, and writing code.

 

Arthur-Louis Heath

Daniel Joffe

I was on the team for writing Thalia's financial analysis code. While I'm first and foremost a programmer, I enjoy other nerdy activities such as speedcubing and D&D. Also power metal.

 

Marcell Veiner

Lastly, my fair share of the project was designing the website and the dashboard. This meant connecting the business and presentation layer of Thalia and visualising the data with responsive graphs, and plots.
